Why people use it

Screen shares & calls

Your bookmarks bar can reveal more than you'd think — banking, job searches, medical portals. Clear it before anyone else sees your screen.

Recordings & demos

Keep tutorials, demos, and screen recordings looking clean and professional without manually rearranging bookmarks first.

Forgot the shortcut?

Ctrl+Shift+B toggles the bar in most browsers — but it doesn't work reliably everywhere, and most people don't remember it anyway.

How it works

1
Click the icon.Every bookmark on your bar moves into a hidden folder. The bar goes empty instantly.
2
Do your call, recording, or screenshot.Nothing on your bar is visible to anyone watching your screen.
3
Click again to restore.Everything moves back to the bar in the exact same order. Nothing is ever deleted.

Support

If something isn't working as expected, note your browser (Chrome, Brave, Edge) and version, and what you were doing when it happened. Hide My Bookmarks only uses Chrome's built-in bookmarks API — nothing is sent off your device.
