Privacy Policy

AI Desktop Corral (Windows & Mac) · Effective July 3, 2026

AI Desktop Corral does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. It has no accounts, no analytics, no ads, and makes no network connections. Everything the app does stays on your own device.

What the app can access, and why

To arrange windows, AI Desktop Corral needs permission to move and resize the windows of other apps. On macOS this is the Accessibility permission; on Windows it uses the standard window APIs. This access is used only to reposition and resize windows. The app does not read the contents of your windows, your keystrokes, or your screen, and it never records or sends any of that information anywhere.

What is stored, and where

Your preferences — things like split ratio, boundary settings, and which side a window belongs on — are saved locally on your own device (in your user preferences on macOS, and in your app data folder on Windows). This information never leaves your machine and is never sent to us or anyone else.

Network and third parties

The app makes no network connections of any kind. There is no telemetry, no crash reporting, no analytics, and no third-party services embedded in it. Nothing about your usage is transmitted off your device.

Children's privacy

The app collects no data from anyone, including children. There is nothing to collect.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date. Because the app collects nothing, any changes are expected to be minor clarifications.
