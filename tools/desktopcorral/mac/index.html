Why it helps

⇥

Drag-to-edge snapping

Drag any window toward the left or right edge and it snaps into that zone. No hotkeys to memorize, no menus to hunt through — just drag and let go.

▥

Two stable work zones

Your AI assistant stays on one side, your browser or doc on the other. Choose the split — 50/50, 60/40, 70/30 — and the layout holds.

⌨

Keyboard shortcuts, menu bar

Send the active window left (⌃⌥←), right (⌃⌥→), or fill its zone (⌃⌥M). Lives quietly in the menu bar — no Dock clutter.

Arranging windows by hand gets old fast.

You drag a window to one side. You resize it. You line up the next one. Then something opens off-center and the whole layout drifts, and you do it all again.

AI Desktop Corral holds a clean two-zone split for you. Drag a window to an edge and it snaps into that zone at the exact size you set. Your AI stays put on one side, your work on the other, and the arrangement stops fighting you.

Getting started

Download and drag to Applications. Unzip the download and drag AI Desktop Corral into your Applications folder, then open it.
Grant Accessibility once. macOS asks for Accessibility permission — this is what lets the app move windows. Turn it on in System Settings and relaunch.
Drag windows into place. Drag a window to the left or right edge to snap it, or use the keyboard shortcuts. Your zones hold while you work.
