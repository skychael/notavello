Koga Browser

Privacy

This policy describes the pre-release Koga Browser and will be updated if the released app behaves differently.

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Data stored on your device

Koga provides browser features including tabs and bookmarks. Information needed to keep those features and app settings available is stored by the app on the Android device. The exact storage behavior may change before release.

Websites and Android WebView

When you visit a website, requests and information you enter are sent directly to that website and its service providers. Pages are displayed using Android WebView. Website cookies, scripts, forms, analytics, advertising, and privacy practices are controlled by the websites and WebView components involved, not by this page.

Downloads and uploads

Files you download are saved to the location handled by Android and the app. Files are uploaded only when you choose a file for a website form; the selected file is then sent to that website.

Android permissions

Koga has not yet been publicly released, so a final verified permission list is not available. Android will display permissions requested by the release build. This policy will be updated before a public download is provided if the final app requires additional explanation.

Contact

Privacy questions can be sent through the Notavello contact page.
