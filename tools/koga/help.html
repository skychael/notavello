Koga Browser

Help & documentation

A quick guide to browsing and adjusting Koga to work the way you prefer.

Browse and navigate

  • Use Back and Forward to move through pages you have visited in the current tab.
  • Enter a web address or search in the address field, then select Go. The same control becomes Refresh or Stop when appropriate.
  • Bare domains such as google.com automatically open using HTTPS.

Tabs, settings, and bookmarks

  • Open Tabs to switch between pages, close tabs, or create a new one.
  • In the Tabs window, select the gear to open Settings.
  • Use Bookmarks to save the current page, revisit saved pages, or remove bookmarks.

Appearance and homepage

In Settings, choose Follow system, Light, or Dark appearance. You can also choose the homepage that opens whenever you create a new tab.

Uploads and downloads

When a website asks for a file, choose the file you want to upload. Downloads are saved through Android and remain available after you clear Koga browsing data.

Clear history and cache

Settings includes an option to clear browsing history and cached page files. Koga asks for confirmation before anything is cleared.

Your open tabs, bookmarks, settings, cookies, signed-in sessions, saved credentials, and downloaded files remain preserved.

Privacy and support

Read the Koga privacy page for details about browsing and device data. If you need assistance or want to report a problem, visit Koga Support.
