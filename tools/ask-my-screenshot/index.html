A helper app, not another account.

Ask My Screenshot is designed to be a lightweight bridge between your screenshots and the AI tools you already use.

It does not answer the question itself, run a cloud AI model, or require you to create a Notavello account.

  • Useful for troubleshooting, explaining screens, drafting replies, summarizing visual information, and asking clearer questions.
  • Works with existing AI services such as ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity.
  • Keeps the workflow focused: screenshot, prompt, open AI, attach from Photos.

Privacy starts with device control.

Your screenshots remain on your iPhone unless you choose to send or attach them somewhere else. Ask My Screenshot prepares text and opens your chosen AI provider.

Important note

When you open an outside AI provider, that provider’s own app, website, account, and privacy terms apply. Ask My Screenshot does not control those third-party services.

  • No Notavello account required.
  • No built-in advertising profile or user tracking inside the app.
  • No screenshot is sent to Notavello by the app.

Simple workflow

Choose a screenshot. Start from a screenshot already saved on your iPhone.
Pick what kind of help you want. Create a clear prompt for troubleshooting, explanation, reply help, summarizing, or general questions.
Open your AI tool and attach the screenshot. The question is ready. To add the image, tap + in the AI app or website and choose the screenshot from Photos.

Support for Ask My Screenshot

If something is not working, include your iPhone model, iOS version, the AI provider you selected, and what happened after tapping Open AI App or Website. Do not send private screenshots unless they are necessary for support.
