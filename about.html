About Notavello

Notavello exists because exporting an AI conversation shouldn't require a workaround.

If you use Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini seriously — for legal research, consulting work, business decisions, or just keeping a record — you've run into the same problem. The conversation is valuable. You want to save it, share it, or file it. But the AI tools don't give you a clean way to do that.

You end up screenshotting pages, printing to PDF with all the user interface chrome included, or copy-pasting into a Word document and spending twenty minutes reformatting. None of it is good enough.

Notavello takes your copy-pasted conversation and turns it into a clean, color-coded PDF in seconds. Your messages in blue. AI responses in plain text. Professional enough to share with a client or file in a case.

It runs entirely in your browser. Your conversation never touches our servers. There's nothing to install, no account required to start, and no limit on how many times you use it for free.

How Notavello was made

Notavello was built through an extended collaboration between a human and an AI — the same kind of AI this tool is designed to export.

Every line of code, every design decision, every word of copy was the result of a real back-and-forth conversation. The human brought the problem, the vision, and the judgment. The AI brought the execution. Neither could have built this alone.

We think that's worth being honest about. The world is filling up with software built this way — tools, products, and companies where the traditional line between creator and creation has quietly blurred. Most of them won't tell you. We will.

What you're using is a product of genuine human intent, shaped and built with AI. We think that's actually something to be proud of — not hidden.
